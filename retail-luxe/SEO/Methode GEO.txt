Before you think about content or backlinks, your website needs to be technically clean. LLMs rely heavily on structured data, crawlability, and clarity.

Checklist

Sitemap exists, is updated, and lists core pages
SSL certificate is valid

Step 2. Strengthen Your Domain Authority
Once your site is technically healthy, LLMs look at authority.

Structuring product pages with complete specifications, FAQs, and references
